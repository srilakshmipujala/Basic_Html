Welcome to SURE Turst,Enhancing the employability of educated unemployed rural youth through Human values-based skill up-gradation in emerging technologies with students.


Quality training for skill upgradation is paramount in a technology dominated world. Rural youth miss out of the initiatives due to remoteness. Hence there is an urgent need to bring the educated unemployed rural youth into the main stream. This online training is available across india.

  • Bridging gap between the skills acquired through academic degrees and the skills required in the industry
  • Mandatory training in Education in Human Values / Life Skills
  • The cost students have to pay for each course is 'discipline, regularity and Punctuality but not money.
